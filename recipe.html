Recipe Name : Tea

Ingredient

  • Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Tea Powder
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Turn On Gas.
  2. Boil half cup of Water.
  3. Crush Ginger and add into water.
  4. Add two table spoon of sugar and one table spoon of Tea.
  5. Add half cup of milk.
  6. Boil the micture.
  7. Pour in a cup.
  8. Tea is ready to drink.
